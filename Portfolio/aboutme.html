Originally from southeast Missouri, I received my BA in Art History from Truman State University, spent an all too brief year in England receiving an MPhil in Education, and currently reside in Boston. I'm an educational publishing representative by day, library science student by night, and a lover books, tacos, and movie theatres at all the times in between.

Through my various research projects, I have found my passion for technological access, community development, and industry engagement in emergent platforms. At FlatWorld Publishing, I am constantly aiming to create a better digital product for our users and build an effective learning community.
